Project Description and Webpage Functionality
Project Overview

The project titled Empowering Networks for Seniors: A Centralized Digital Resource Platform for Older Adults in Forsyth County aims to enhance access to essential community-based services for older adults in Winston-Salem and Forsyth County. The region offers a wide array of services—including meal assistance, transportation, healthcare referrals, housing support, employment programs, and social engagement opportunities—through various nonprofit organizations, government agencies, and community groups.

Despite the availability of these resources, access remains a significant challenge due to the fragmented nature of information distribution. Currently, service details are spread across multiple websites, printed directories, PDF documents, and phone-based systems. This lack of centralization creates confusion and inefficiencies for older adults, caregivers, and even service providers attempting to coordinate care or make referrals.

Additionally, many older adults face barriers such as limited digital literacy, accessibility needs, and difficulty navigating complex or inconsistent information systems. These challenges can prevent individuals from accessing critical services that support their independence, health, and overall quality of life.

To address these issues, this project proposes the development of a centralized, user-friendly digital platform that consolidates relevant resources into a single, easily accessible location. The platform is designed with a strong emphasis on simplicity, clarity, and accessibility to ensure it meets the needs of older adult users. By improving access to information and enhancing coordination among service providers, the project seeks to strengthen community support systems and reduce service gaps throughout the county.

Webpage Functionality

The proposed digital platform will incorporate several key features to ensure usability, accessibility, and effectiveness for its intended audience.

The core component of the platform is a centralized resource directory, which will serve as a comprehensive database of available services within Forsyth County. Resources will be organized into clearly defined categories such as food and meal assistance, transportation services, healthcare and medical support, housing and financial assistance, employment and volunteer opportunities, and social or recreational activities. Each resource listing will include essential details such as a description of services, eligibility requirements, contact information, and hours of operation.

To enhance usability, the platform will feature an intuitive search and filter system that allows users to quickly locate relevant services. Users will be able to search by keywords and refine results based on service type, location, and eligibility criteria. This functionality is particularly important for older adults who may need quick and straightforward access to information without navigating complex menus.

The platform will also prioritize a user-friendly interface tailored to the needs of older adults. Design elements will include large, readable text, high-contrast color schemes, and a simplified navigation structure. The website will be fully responsive, ensuring accessibility across multiple devices, including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

In addition, accessibility features will be integrated to support users with varying abilities. These features will include compatibility with screen readers, options for text enlargement, and the use of plain language to ensure clarity and comprehension. The goal is to minimize barriers and create an inclusive digital environment.

Each service listed in the directory will have a dedicated resource detail page, providing users with in-depth information on how to access services, apply for assistance, and connect directly with providers. This ensures transparency and reduces confusion when navigating available options.

The platform will also include a community updates and announcements section, where users can stay informed about local events, program changes, and time-sensitive resources. This feature helps keep the platform dynamic and relevant to current community needs.

Finally, a help and support section will be available to guide users through the platform. This will include step-by-step instructions, frequently asked questions, and contact options for additional assistance. A potential future enhancement includes a secure partner portal that would allow organizations to update their own information, ensuring the platform remains accurate and up to date over time.
